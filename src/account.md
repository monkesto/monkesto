# Account

An account represents a named asset or liability with a value that can be tracked over time. For example, a bank account, credit card, or accounts payable are all examples of accounts.

Transaction entries may be associated with an account, and when they are they affect the accumulated balance of the account. Entries are not directly owned by an account, but are owned by the transaction and are associated to an account.

The accumulated balance of an account is respective to a particular journal. The balance of each account starts at zero, and changes as transaction entries are added that include the account.
